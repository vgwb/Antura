## Game Design Notes

**Mission Objective**
Find Giotto in order to obtain the keys to the bell tower and gain access to it.


**Characters**

1. **Tower Guard**: After learning that Giotto’s bell tower is closed, the player goes to the guard to ask for information. The guard explains that they need to find Giotto, as he holds the keys to the tower.
2. **Color Seller**: The player speaks with the nearest NPC to gather clues about Giotto’s whereabouts. The color seller suggests following a trail of paint that Giotto has left along the streets.
3. **Girl**: By following the trail of colors, the player encounters a young girl. She raises questions about Giotto’s art. After the player helps clear her doubts, she mentions that she saw Giotto speaking with someone.
4. **Messenger**: The player reaches the messenger. After solving his problem, he reveals that Giotto has recently gone to his own bell tower.
5. **Giotto**: Finally, the player finds Giotto. Before handing over the key, he challenges the player with several questions to test whether they truly know him.


### Learning Goals
Learn how Giotto changed the way painting was done by making the figures he depicted more realistic compared to the painters of his time. Also learn that Giotto was not only a skilled painter but also an architect.
