## Game Design Notes

**Mission Objective**
Visit the museum and answer a hysterical old man's questions correctly to calm him down.

**Characters**
- **MrBaffo**: A gentleman who introduces the player to the quest and asks for help calming the hysterical old man.
- **Archaeologist**: An extremely angry elderly man who can't stand the fact that no one knows anything about the Etruscans. The player must convince him otherwise to complete the quest.
- **Museum Secretary**: An NPC the player must interact with to enter the museum.
- **Museum Guard**: Introduces the bronze statuettes task. To view one of the essential cards for the old man's quiz, the player must first collect a series of bronze statuettes.

### Learning Goals
Learn four fundamental concepts that characterize the Etruscans.

### Cards
- Museum
- Archaeology
- Etruscan people
- Etruscan influence on the Romans
- Necropolis
- Etruscan metals
- Etruscan cities
- Etruscan religion
- Etruscan writing
- Etruscan trade
- Etruscan women

### Gameplay Structure
- Quest introduction with MrBaffo and the old man (Archeologist)
- Museum entrance (count the money activity, easy difficulty)
- Showcase of the most important cards as rewards of 3 activities and 1 task
    - Etruscan territory and period (Canvas activity, easy difficulty, starts by interacting with an object on display)
    - Etruscan influence on the Romans (Puzzle activity, easy/medium difficulty, starts by interacting with an object on display)
    - Necropolis (Canvas activity, medium difficulty, starts by interacting with an object on display)
    - Etruscan metals (Items collection task, starts by interacting with the museum guard)
        - Interact with the guard, collect the 6 statues spreaded in the museum, return to the guard and interact with the statues on display to read the last required card
- Ols man's final quiz: verification of the knowledge acquired by reading the previous 4 cards
- Drop of many cookies as a quest completition reward

### Places
- San Casciano Val di Pesa (environment's town)
- Ghelli museum (place where the main part of the quest takes part)
- Archer's tomb in Sant'Angelo (necropoli represented into the "necropolis" card)
