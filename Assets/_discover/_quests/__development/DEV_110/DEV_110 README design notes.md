**Mission Objective**
Help Dante traverse the dark forest.

**Characters**

Dante : Player helps dante traversing the dark forest.
He will flee upon seeing the beasts in the forest and the Player will need to help dante escape.

Virgil : The true guide of Dante in his Divina Commedia. 
He guides Dante to escape from the dark forest and joins him during the last part where the beasts stops Dante in his tracks again.

Three Beasts : 
Lynx - Sin of Lust.
Lion - Sin of Pride.
She-Wolf - Sin of Avarice. 

### Learning Goals
The player will learn the development of the events inside the first canto, while having access to the structure via cards.

### Gameplay Structure
The player helps Dante and moves around the dark forest to escape it.
Fight structure: 
The player has access to a menu where he can [ ACT ] via acting the player can choose dialogues options ( which can trigger "standard" activities )  upon choosing the right one the fight ends.

### Places
Dante's dark forest.

### Final Assessment
This is a short experience that will bring in more attention to the Opera while giving little bits of Knowledge that can be well integrated by Teachers while playing.
