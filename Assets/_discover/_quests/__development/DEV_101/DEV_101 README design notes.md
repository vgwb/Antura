## Game Design Notes

**Mission Objective**
Help Guido d'Arezzo recover all notes fragments shattered through the city.

**Characters**

1. **Guido Monaco d'Arezzo**: Antura shattered the pentagram and he is searching for all the fragments throughout the city. He asked for the players' help and guides them through Arezzo, giving information about the city.
2. **Cantore**: Chief of the choir, he will receive the music sheet when the players collect all the music notes.

### Learning Goals
1. Learn who invented music notation
2. What is the musical pentagram
3. What is a treble clef
4. What are the musical notes
5. Where are the notes written
