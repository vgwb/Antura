## Game Design Notes FR_10

**Mission Statement** The player explores the banks of the River Seine in Paris to help a distressed Tourist recover 7 missing guidebook photos stolen by Antura. By interacting with "River Friends" and local experts, the player learns to identify different types of bridges and boats, eventually discovering where the river flows.

### Characters

- **The Tourist:** The quest-giver who has lost their Paris guidebook pages.
- **River Friends & Experts:** NPCs stationed near landmarks who provide specific facts and guard the collectible photo chests.
- **Spawned Residents:** Local Parisians providing atmospheric flavor and random river facts.

### Knowledge Content

- **Bridges of the Seine:** There are 37 bridges in total. Recognition of three main types:
- **Bridges for cars:** Fast-moving transit requiring safe crossings.
- **Footbridges (Passerelles):** Exclusive for pedestrians.
- **Bridges for trains/metros:** Built to be exceptionally strong.
- **Pont Alexandre III:** Recognition of this iconic bridge with its gold statues.


- **Boat Types:**
- **Bâteaux-mouches:** Tourist boats with large windows for sightseeing.
- **Barges (Péniches):** Houseboats where people live.
- **Goods Boats:** Industrial vessels used to transport cargo and boxes.


- **Geography:** The Seine flows North through France and empties into the **English Channel**.

### Gameplay Flow

#### Step 0: The Arrival (Tutorial)

- **Location:** The banks of the Seine.
- **Action:** The Tourist explains that Antura took the guidebook. The player must first complete a tutorial task (**collect_cards**) to find the initial missing pages.

#### Step 1: The Search for 7 Photos

- **Location:** Open exploration along the river.
- **Requirement:** The player must find 7 specific NPCs guarding chests.
- **Action:** Talk to the NPC -> Answer a specific question about the landmark -> Solve a mini-game (Jigsaw, Memory, or Canvas) to unlock the chest.

- **The 7 Collectibles:**
1. **Boat for Goods** (Question: What does it carry?)
2. **House Boat** (Question: Why do people stay here?)
3. **Tourist Boat** (Question: Why the big windows?)
4. **Pont Alexandre III** (Question: What makes it famous?)
5. **Car Bridge** (Question: How do you cross safely?)
6. **Train Bridge** (Question: What must it be like?)
7. **Footbridge** (Question: What is it called?)

#### Step 2: The Final Geography Lesson

- **Requirement:** Collect all 7 photo cards and return to the Tourist.
- **Action:** A final conversation with the Tourist triggers a geography check.
- **Quiz:** "The Seine flows into a sea. Which one?" (Correct Answer: **The English Channel**).

#### Step 3: The Grand Finale

- **Action:** The Tourist is happy and ready to go home.
- **Reward:** Quest complete! Option to do a post-quest activity (Drawing a bridge in a notebook).
